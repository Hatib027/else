Manual trading refers to the process of buying and selling financial instruments, such as stocks, currencies, or commodities, by a human trader without relying on automated trading systems or algorithms. AI trading, also known as algorithmic trading or automated trading, involves the use of artificial intelligence (AI) and sophisticated algorithms to make trading decisions on financial markets.
